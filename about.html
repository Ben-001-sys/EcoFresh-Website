About EcoFresh

EcoFresh was created with one mission: to make everyday cleaning safer for people and the planet. Our products are made using plant‑based ingredients that clean effectively without harmful chemicals.

We believe sustainability should be simple, affordable, and accessible. Every EcoFresh product is designed to reduce environmental impact while keeping homes fresh and healthy.
